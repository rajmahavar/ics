News

PG assembly 2015

The second PG Assembly of college, 2015 was held at Vardhman Mahavir Medical College & Safdurjung hospital, New Delhi from 01 Feb to 07 Feb 2015. Next PG assembly 2016 will be held in February at G B Pant Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education and Research, New Delhi in February 2016.

Accreditation for the fellowship courses

ICA started accrediting fellowship courses and accredited programmes at Max Saket Hospital, New Delhi on Onco Anaesthesia and Ultrasound guided Pain management procedures. First batch of students completed the course in June 2015. More accredited courses will be started at Capital Hospitals in 2016in various subspecialty of Anaesthesia.

Guidelines

Guidelines on Anaesthesia practice was released during the midterm conference 2015.

Convocation conference 2015

The midterm conference and convocation ICA 2015 was held at G.B. Pant Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education and Research, New Delhi on 4-5 July 2015. Pre conference workshops were conducted at SGRH, VMC & SJH, Max- Saket,Delhi and Medanta Medicity Hospitals in Gurgaon with overwhelming responses.

New divisions

Very soon College will bring in Anaesthesia Patient safety foundation and ambulatory anaesthesia care division.
