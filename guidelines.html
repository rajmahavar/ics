Guidelines

Guidelines Books

Salient Features

  • The first volume of Practice Guidelines.
  • This is a unique effort by Indian College of Anaesthesia (ICA) an academic wing of Indian Society of Anaesthesiologists.
  • It contains 23 up-to-date topics that cover the current practice guidelines for preoperative preparations, perioperative problems and their management during anaesthesia, pain management and intensive care therapy.
  • This book is recommended for postgraduate students, trainees, practicing anaesthesiologists and those involved in care of critically ill.
  • The readers would find it beneficial in managing the patients that require anesthetic care, intensive therapy and pain management.
